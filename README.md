# Vestora 📈

> AI-powered retail investor intelligence platform for Uganda's capital markets.

## What is Vestora?

Vestora is a smart, mobile-first financial technology platform that makes Uganda's capital markets accessible to every citizen — not just institutions and professionals.

Built for the Uganda Securities Exchange (USE) ecosystem, Vestora bridges the gap between complex financial systems and ordinary Ugandan investors through real-time market intelligence, AI-powered explanations, portfolio tracking, and interactive financial education.

## The Problem

Less than 5% of Ugandans participate in capital markets. Not because they lack the means — but because the tools were never built for them.

- Market data is inaccessible and hard to interpret
- No AI-powered tools exist for retail investors locally
- Financial literacy infrastructure is almost nonexistent
- Existing platforms serve institutions, not citizens

## The Solution

Vestora puts a personal financial intelligence assistant in every Ugandan's pocket.

- **Market Dashboard** — real-time USE market overview in plain language
- **AI Financial Assistant** — ask any investment question, get a simple answer
- **Portfolio Tracker** — monitor holdings, returns, and dividend performance
- **Investor Education Hub** — learn investing from scratch, at your own pace
- **Smart Alerts** — never miss a price movement or dividend announcement

## Built With

- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS, Recharts
- **Backend:** FastAPI, Python
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **AI:** OpenAI API
- **Deployment:** Docker

## Status

Active development — MVP targeting June 2026.

Submitted to the **Uganda Ministry of ICT and National Guidance** Government Systems Prototype Showcase and National Innovator Registry.

## Vision

To become East Africa's leading retail investor intelligence platform — starting with Uganda, expanding across the region.
